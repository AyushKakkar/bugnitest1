About Bugni!

Bugni is a financial education startup aimed at helping children develop good saving habits from a young age. The company provides an app and a paper savings account for kids, which allows them to learn about managing money through a fun and interactive experience. This will help them develop good financial habits that will benefit them in the future. Parents can set up allowance, chores, and reward systems for their children through the app, and kids can save and track their earnings. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and engaging, making it easy for kids to track and see the potential growth of their savings over time. Additionally, Bugni offers educational resources like articles and videos, and games to teach children about budgeting, saving, and investing. The goal of the company is to empower the next generation with the financial skills they need to succeed in the future.
